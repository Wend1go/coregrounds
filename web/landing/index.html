INTRODUCTION

Unique PVP tower defense gameplay

Forget everything you know about tower defense games. In Coregrounds you and your opponent fight head-to-head, on one battlefield.
Build a maze of walls, place and move towers, spawn minions, use abilities, and upgrade your units at the right time to destroy your opponent's core!

MOBA LIKE DRAFT PHASE

You and your opponent take turns banning and picking. You each pick 5 units (minions or towers), and 3 abilities. Create epic combos, or find counters for your opponent's choices.

Play to win, pay to skin

You can unlock all units, abilities and modifications just by playing the game. Use the premium currency to buy skins, colors and more.
